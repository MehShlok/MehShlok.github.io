About Me

Hi there! I'm Shlok Mehendale, a dual-degree student at BITS Pilani, KK Birla Goa Campus, pursuing BE in Computer Science and MSc in Mathematics.


Passionate about the convergence of tech and math, I'm particularly drawn to machine learning and artificial intelligence. I have recently also developed an interest in Neuroscience, NeuroAI and consciousness studies particularly. Outside the classroom, I'm deeply involved in hands-on Machine Learning projects, exploring both theory and practical applications. I'm also a part of Project Kratos through which I enjoy venturing into applying Deep Learning in Robotics, as showcased in the Projects section. Apart from that, I'm also excited about the continuous advancements in Theoretical Mathematics.


Apart from this, I love Music (feeling of a deeper connection with mathematics) and in my free time you'll find me on the badminton court, chessboard or playing Guitar!


Explore my projects and connect if you share similar interests or have exciting ideas to discuss!
